# Shared CloudWatch Log Groups

## Purpose

Centralized logging enables consistent monitoring and troubleshooting across enterprise workloads.

## Log Groups

- `/shared/services/application`
- `/shared/services/security`
- `/shared/services/platform`

## Retention

Recommended lab retention: `30 days`

## Enterprise Value

Shared log groups provide centralized visibility, troubleshooting support, audit support, operational consistency, and future alarm/dashboard integration.
